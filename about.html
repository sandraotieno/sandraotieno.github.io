ABOUT ME

Who I am

I am a data analytics and business intelligence professional with more than six years of experience helping organisations interpret their markets, customers and performance. I have worked across banking, FMCG, telecoms, retail, NGOs and development programs in multiple African markets.

My strength is understanding the business context behind each question and shaping analytical approaches that decision makers can trust. I like working with analysts, engineers and leadership teams so that the technical work always connects back to real trade offs and choices.

I hold a Master of Science in Data Science and Analytics and a Bachelor of Economics and Statistics. This mix of analytical depth and business grounding allows me to move comfortably between statistical work, modelling, BI development and strategic reporting.

My focus is on clarity, accuracy and insight that moves from data to understanding to stories that leaders can act on with confidence.

How I do it
  • 1. Shape data collection and structure I design or refine survey instruments, sampling plans and input templates, and map how data should land in tables so that later analysis is straightforward.
  • 2. Clean, prepare and validate data I use SQL, Python, R and spreadsheet tools to clean, join and check data, creating stable ELT and ETL outputs that are ready for modelling and BI.
  • 3. Explore and model I run exploratory analysis and build statistical and machine learning models for segmentation, forecasting, classification, credit and behavioural questions.
  • 4. Build BI structures and metric layers I design star schemas, metric layers and ELT / ETL workflows with SQL, dbt and Power Query, supporting dashboards and reports in Power BI, Tableau and Looker Studio.
  • 5. Tell the story I translate results into visual narratives, concise summaries and executive presentations that explain what the numbers show, why they matter and which decisions they support.
