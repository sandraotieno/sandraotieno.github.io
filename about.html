About

I sit at the intersection of Data Analytics, Business Intelligence and Insight Generation – taking raw, multi-source data and turning it into models, dashboards and stories that help leaders make confident decisions.

Who I am

I am a data analytics professional with 5+ years of experience in data modelling, insight generation and business-focused analysis across multiple African markets. I design ELT/ETL pipelines, build metric layers, and transform raw survey, operational and financial data into analytical models that answer complex commercial, customer and product questions.

My work spans market insight, customer analytics and executive reporting for organisations in banking, FMCG, telecoms, retail, NGOs and development programs. I am comfortable moving between SQL and Python, BI tools and stakeholder rooms – acting as a bridge between engineering, analytics and business teams to ensure that data is reliable, interpretable and directly tied to real decisions.

How I work
  • 1. Structure & ingest data
    Clean and combine data from surveys, operational systems or files into analysis-ready tables using SQL, Python, Power Query and ELT/ETL workflows.
  • 2. Explore & model
    Run exploratory analysis, build statistical and machine learning models (classification, forecasting, segmentation) and quantify patterns that matter for performance and risk.
  • 3. Build BI layers
    Create data models, measures and visuals in Power BI / Tableau / Looker Studio, aligning KPIs and drill-down views with concrete business questions.
  • 4. Communicate insight
    Translate results into concise narratives, dashboards and executive decks that focus on the “so what”, options, and next steps for decision-makers.
