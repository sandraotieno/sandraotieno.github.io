FROM DATA COLLECTION TO DECISION-READY STORIES.

I work along the full data journey – connecting how information is collected, structured, analysed and finally communicated in the rooms where decisions are made.

Who I am

I am a data analytics and business intelligence professional with over five years of experience working with organisations across African markets. My background spans market insight, customer analytics and performance reporting for banking, FMCG, telecoms, retail, NGOs and development programs.

I enjoy translating between technical and non-technical teams – understanding business questions, working with analysts and engineers on the data, and then shaping outputs that leaders can use confidently. My focus is always on clarity: reliable numbers, simple stories and concrete options for action.

How I work
  • 1. Design & collect data
    Collaborate on survey instruments, sampling frames and data-collection workflows, and connect to operational, financial or customer systems so that inputs are structured and traceable.
  • 2. Structure & model
    Clean, transform and combine raw data into analysis-ready tables; build statistical and machine learning models (classification, forecasting, segmentation) to quantify behaviour, performance and risk.
  • 3. Build BI layers
    Create data models, measures and visuals in Power BI, Tableau and Looker Studio, translating business questions into KPIs, drill-downs and monitoring views.
  • 4. Report & communicate
    Package results into dashboards and Microsoft Office outputs (Excel analysis packs, Word summaries and PowerPoint decks) that highlight the “so what”, options and next steps for decision-makers.
