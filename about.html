From raw data to meaningful, decision-ready stories

I work across the full analytical process, from how data is collected and prepared to how it is modelled, visualised and communicated in a way that supports sound, confident decision making.

Who I am

I am a data analytics and business intelligence professional with more than six years of experience translating raw information into insight that guides commercial, operational and strategic decisions. My experience spans banking, FMCG, telecoms, retail, NGOs and development programs across African markets.

I bring together technical skill and business understanding. I interpret questions from stakeholders, work with analysts and engineers to shape the right data foundations and deliver outputs that leaders can trust. My approach centres on clarity, accuracy and stories that highlight what matters and why.

What I do

I support the complete analytics lifecycle beginning with the design of survey instruments and structured data collection. I clean, validate and prepare raw data using SQL, Python, R, SPSS, Stata and Power Query to create reliable, analysis-ready datasets.

I explore and model data using statistical analysis, segmentation, forecasting, classification and behavioural modelling. I apply tools such as Pandas, Polars, PySpark, Scikit-Learn and statistical procedures across R, SPSS and Stata to quantify relationships and uncover patterns that matter.

I build BI-ready data structures using SQL, Power Query and dbt, then design dashboards and performance views in Power BI, Tableau and Looker Studio. My work focuses on clear KPIs, intuitive layouts and metrics that reflect how organisations operate.

I bring everything together through concise narratives, visual stories and executive summaries that explain what the numbers mean, why the results matter and which decisions they support. My goal is always insight that moves from data to understanding to stories that lead to action.
