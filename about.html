About

I work at the intersection of Data Analytics, Business Intelligence and Insight Generation – taking raw, messy data from surveys, operations or finance and turning it into models, dashboards and decision-ready stories for stakeholders.

Who I am

I am a Data Analytics and Business Intelligence professional with over 5 years of experience working across African markets. My work spans cleaning and preparing datasets, building analytical models, structuring ETL/ELT workflows and designing dashboards that senior teams can act on.

I enjoy moving between code, BI tools and stakeholder conversations. I am comfortable exploring data in Python and SQL, modelling behaviour and risk, and then packaging findings into executive decks, dashboards and short analytical briefs.

How I work
  • 1. Structure & ingest data
    Clean and combine data from surveys, operational systems or files into analysis-ready tables using SQL, Python, Power Query and ETL/ELT pipelines.
  • 2. Explore & model
    Run exploratory analysis, build statistical or machine learning models (classification, forecasting, segmentation) and quantify patterns that matter for decisions.
  • 3. Build BI layers
    Create data models, measures and visuals in Power BI/Tableau/Looker Studio, aligning metrics and filters with business questions.
  • 4. Communicate insight
    Translate findings into concise narratives, executive decks and PDF reports that focus on “so what” and clear options for decision-makers.
