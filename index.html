About Me & Skills

I work end-to-end across the analytics lifecycle: structuring data, building models, designing BI layers and communicating the story behind the numbers.

I am a Data Analytics and Business Intelligence professional with more than five years of experience working with multi-country data across African markets. I enjoy cleaning and structuring messy data, building analytical models and dashboards, and translating results into clear recommendations for banks, FMCG brands, retailers and development partners.

My background combines data science, consumer and market insight, and structured reporting. I am comfortable moving between SQL, Python, BI tools and stakeholder discussions to keep analysis grounded in real business questions.
