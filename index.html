Data Analytics

Data analytics is the systematic exploration of raw data using statistical methods, coding, and algorithmic techniques to uncover trends, patterns, and relationships. It transforms large and small datasets into measurable evidence that explains what happened, why it happened, and what is likely to happen next. Data analytics spans descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive analysis, enabling teams to quantify behaviour, model scenarios, and support data-driven actions across products, operations, and strategy.

Data Analytics Python, R, SQL, SPSS, Stata, Jupyter Notebook.
Business Intelligence Power BI, Tableau, Looker Studio, DAX, Power Query, ETL/ELT data models.
Insight Generation Excel, PowerPoint, survey analysis frameworks and reporting templates for executive decks and PDFs.
